Free video clip

A free video clip, encoded into a variety of formats and resolutions to easily run tests in your next project.

Why?

Because when I need a free video clip (whitout license/restrictions), I can just pick it up here. And now you can do the same! :)

About the footage

  • The video show Oslo Opera House, shot from a tripod.
  • The audio is just ambient noise from the city.
  • The source video/audio is shot on a Fujifilm X-E1 (so unfortunately the source quality is not excellent).
  • Why text overlay? To easily see what clip is running during a test.

License?

  • Free, do whatever you want.
    And if you use it for something awesome... feel free to let me know :)

Encoding

  • Settings for encoding was picked at random/default - do NOT use for comparison of codecs.
  • .mp4 files is encoded using Adobe Media Encoder (YouTube Presets).
  • .webm and .ogv files is encoded using Firefogg (Standard presets).
